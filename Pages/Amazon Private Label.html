Amazon Private Label

What is Amazon Private Label ?

Amazon Private Labels is about finding a product on Amazon that has high sales, but maybe a low number of reviews; getting that product, and labeling it as if it were yours. With retail arbitrage, you go to retail stores to find products on a deep discount; you buy that product; look for its original price, and sell it on Amazon to make a profit. In the long run, this practice can turn into a real hassle since you have to be constantly going out to hunt those bargains.
